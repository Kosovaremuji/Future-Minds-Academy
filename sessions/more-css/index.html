Tools tailor-made

For Team meetings

WireSquare enables you to share your audio, slides, chat, video, and desktop with your team. Built-in polling makes it easy to engage your team, and recording your lectures helps you can make them available for later review.

Live whiteboard

For presenters and groups

When using the whiteboard tool in WireSquare, annotations are automatically displayed back to your team in real-time. Presenters also have the ability to zoom, highlight, draw and write on presentations, making your points clearer to remote attendees
